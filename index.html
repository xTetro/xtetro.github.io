About Me

I'm a hobbyist photographer that has been taking photos of scenery for as long as I've owned a camera. I recently have been utilizing the high quality cameras found in the most recent iPhones, which allow me to take photos when and where ever the opportunity presents itself. I do not use any special apps, but I do edit all my photos within the native iPhone photo and camera apps. I want to share the natural beauty that I have seen, with others, through my photography.
